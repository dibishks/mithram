Mithram

MITHRAM is a registered charitable society (Regn.No.ER.561/93) founded in 1993 by a group of parents of mentally challenged persons with the objective of working to achieve total care, protection, education, vocational training and rehabilitation of persons with Autism, Cerebral palsy. Intellectual and multiple disabilities.

Vision

To provide impetus for the full flowering of a child’s personality by promoting self advocacy.

Mission

  1. To support individuals having disabilities and their families
  2. To achieve social inclusion for living with dignity in the society
  3. To ensure social security
  4. To create a disable friendly social environment.

Awareness

Mental retardation is a condition and not a disease. Early intervention and training can improve outcomes.

Our Services

It was part of our mission to set up a complex with necessary service centers as a model institution which could be an inspiration to parents of other localities to initiate movements like ours so that welfare activities would reach the entire intellectually affected in the country in due course

Residential Care Homes

There are two Residential Care Homes one each for men and women.Admission to care home is restricted to adults (Above 18 years old). At present 38 persons are utilizing the services of care homes for prolonged stay and respite care. The residents are well cared and are enrolled in Mithram Special School and they have a fully engaged routine.

Special school

The mentally challenged are taught according to their individual capacities. More stress is given in making these persons capable to lead their life independently or with minimum dependence. Admission to special school is restricted to the age of 18 years. We have our own school bus.We have a playground and facilities for physiotherapy, yoga, exercise etc.

Saksharatha Mission

Saksharatha Mission of education adults, Mithram provides training, Academics and Computer for better understanding, Certificates from the Govt. of Kerala is issued for the passed students. Computer training is provided for data entry operation.
